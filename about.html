About us

Get to know us a little bit more.

Our little family started out as a tiny bakery from home.

The owner of the restaurant Kelly, began her journey when she was very young, baking goodies at home and selling them at school to her classmates. Later on she began to advertise her baked good along with meals to her community to expand her venture. Once she had saved up enough money, she invested in renting out a small shop which has now become her restaurants home. We have roughly 15 employees as the restaurant is still rather small, however as it continues to grow, we will be on the hunt for a much larger area to rent and possibly purchase to create a solid home for our little family as it expands. We do hope you will join us on this exciting adventure!

Our Story

Our History

Kelly's Kitchen was established in 2018 when our founder Kelly Thompson turned her passion for baking into a small home business. What started as weekend bake sales quickly grew into a catering service for local events. In 2020, despite the challenges of the pandemic, we opened our first brick-and-mortar location in Kempton Park, offering takeaway services that became a neighborhood favorite. By 2022, we expanded to full-service dining, creating the warm and inviting space you experience today.

Our Mission

At Kelly's Kitchen, our mission is to create memorable dining experiences through exceptional food, warm hospitality, and a welcoming atmosphere. We believe that great meals bring people together, and we strive to be the place where families and friends gather to celebrate life's moments, both big and small.

Our Values

  • Quality: We source the freshest ingredients and prepare everything with care and attention to detail.
  • Community: We support local suppliers and actively participate in community events and initiatives.
  • Sustainability: We're committed to reducing our environmental footprint through responsible practices.
  • Innovation: We continuously explore new flavors and techniques while honoring traditional recipes.
  • Inclusivity: We create a welcoming space where everyone feels valued and respected.

Meet Our Team

Kelly Thompson

Founder & Head Chef

Kelly's culinary journey began in her grandmother's kitchen at the age of 8. After completing her training at the Culinary Institute of South Africa, she worked in several high-end restaurants before following her dream of opening Kelly's Kitchen. Her signature dishes combine classic techniques with innovative twists, reflecting her belief that food should be both comforting and exciting.

Michael Naidoo

Executive Chef

With over 15 years of experience in fine dining establishments across South Africa, Chef Michael brings expertise in both local and international cuisines. He specializes in creating seasonal menus that showcase the best local ingredients while incorporating global flavors.

Sarah Molefe

Pastry Chef

Sarah's passion for pastry began during her childhood in Cape Town. After training in France, she returned to South Africa to create the delectable desserts that have become a hallmark of Kelly's Kitchen. Her creations blend European techniques with South African flavors.

David Khumalo

Restaurant Manager

With a background in hospitality management and a warm, welcoming personality, David ensures that every guest at Kelly's Kitchen receives exceptional service. He leads our front-of-house team with a commitment to creating memorable dining experiences.
